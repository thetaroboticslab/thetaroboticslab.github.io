Welcome to Theta Robotics

Theta Robotics is a Robotics and Automation Comapny based in Pune, Maharashtra.
Theta Robotics is primarily a Research and Development Foundation working in Field of Robotics, Internet of things and Embedded System.

What We Do ?

R & D Services

We do Research and development in the field of robotics, Internet of Things and embedded system.

Customised Robots

We design and manufacture customised robots to suit your requirements.
We also provide programming and software solutions for all kind of robots

Robotic Research Platforms

We Design and Develop robotic research platform that includes Bio-Inspired Robots, Mobile Robots, Robotic Arm, Drones and so on, for Institutes, Research Centres, Students and individuals.

Embedded Systems and IoT

We provide one-stop solutions for embedded products, PCB design and manufacturing, Electronic manufacturing, Development Board and Internet of Things

Product Development

We work with companies to drive innovation and introduce cutting-edge devices that positively impact our lives. We solve complex engineering challenges and develop products that transform brands and accelerate advances in technology. 

Robotics Lab Solutions

We Help students to build Robots and Develop innovative student community.
We invole students in research and development with technical assistance.
